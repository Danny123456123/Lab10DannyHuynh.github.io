SEO Techniques

Backlink SEO is a useful strategy that could be employed to enhance position and recognition of a site on the best search engines. Backlinks are the way of telling search engines that a site is worthy of being visited and that it has links from other reputable sites. This endorsement raises a website ‘s awareness since search engines employ backlinks as one of the best factors to consider. Backlinks serve their purpose of enhancing the website ranking while also providing additional traffic sources that increase utilization and accessibility (Silva, 2024).

Another SEO technique is Target Keywords that ensures that a website’s content is providing what the users are searching for. Companies use high volume, relevant keywords on business content that ensures they obtain a better rank on the search engine result pages. This makes it easier for the search engines to identify the topic of the page and ranks well especially to a particular search term. In general, keyword targeting is crucial when it comes to producing organic traffic and improving embed user interest (Silva, 2024).

Appealing to the User’s Browsing Experience is a technique of assessing top competing pages and understanding which tactics are beneficial to choose. By analyzing when and how they use the keyword, the structure of their content, and the backlinking techniques employed, one can mimic these strategies and make one’s website more SEO-friendly.

SMO Techniques

Social media optimization better known as SMO is another successful marketing technique which is used by companies who are willing their social profile north in social sites such as facebook, instagram, tik tok. Through the use of social media platforms, clicks to business websites can be expected while at the same time creating awareness of new products and services. Some common platforms which businesses avail include the popular social media management tools like Hootsuite and Buffer which make it easier to plan for your content and create engagement with the intended audience (Kenton, 2023).

Another SMO strategy is viral marketing, where businesses develop contents that are intended to go viral on social media platforms. This method uses user engagement to amplify the content getting it across to the wider community especially inforum where information goes round faster for instance Twitter and Instagram (Kenton, 2023). The major advantage of viral marketing is that a brand can reach many more people than its fans, essentially those who follow the brand on social media.

Another SMO strategy that can be pointed out is the placement of the “like” and “share” buttons on websites. This kind of button helps users to share and like most posts within their social platform to pull traffic back to the company's website (Kenton, 2023). With such interactions, businesses are able to have more brand awareness, higher chances of conversations with the website visitors and possibly their customers.
